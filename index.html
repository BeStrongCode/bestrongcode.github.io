Research

Low-level vision connects sensors, image degradation, and higher-level visual understanding. I study real-world image quality problems and restoration methods that are interpretable, reproducible, and deployable.

Image Restoration and Enhancement

Denoising, deblurring, deraining, low-light enhancement, and HDR reconstruction for improving visual quality and detail fidelity under challenging imaging conditions.

Super-Resolution and Detail Modeling

Single-image, multi-frame, and real-world super-resolution, with attention to texture recovery, edge structure, and perceptual quality.

Computational Imaging Models

Combining physical imaging priors, data-driven models, and differentiable optimization to reason about noise, blur, exposure, and sensor response.
